The Village of Bishopton

Bishopton (Scottish Gaelic - Baile an Easbaig) is a village located in Renfrewshire, in the west of Scotland, approximately 10 miles from Glasgow.

There is one train station in Bishopton on the Inverclyde line, going between Glasgow Central and Gourock/Wemyss Bay. There is also a bus service going to Clydebank, Erskine and Greenock. Additionally, the village is located 4 miles from Glasgow Airport.

Notable people from Bishopton include:

  • The Baroness Annabell Goldie - Minister of State for Defence and former leader of the Scottish Conservative Party
  • Douglass Alexander - former MP and Foreign Secretary
