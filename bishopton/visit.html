Visit Bishopton

Mar Hall

Mar Hall (or Earl of Mar Hotel) is a luxury golf and spa resort situated just outside of Bishopton. The building was originally Erskine House, and was commissioned to be built by the 11th Lord Blantyre. In 2004, it was converted into a hotel.

Blantyre Monument
image source - Stephen Sweeney

This monument was built to commemerate Robert Walter Stuart, the 11th Lord Blantyre. He served in the Napoleonic wars but was killed in Brussels in 1830.

Dargavel House
image source - Raymond Okonski

Dargavel house is a grade B listed building situated within the Royal Ordnance Factory Site. It was once owned by the entrepreneur Edward Steinkopff.
